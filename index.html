What is an anime season?

While back in the old days of anime, episodes were released weekly for a series, sometimes spanning years, this has largely been phased out nowadays in exchange for an anime to have a set amount of episodes instead. The episode count is traditionally 12-13 episodes for one cour, which lasts around 3 months or a season, hence the seasonal names.
